Overview

  • originated in the United States
  • one rank behind soccer among participation sports

Set-up

  • two teams
  • court divided by a net

Objective

  • send the ball over and ground it on the opponent’s court
  • protect your court

How to Start

  • right back-row player serves

Rules

  • maximum 3 touches per team to send the ball over the net
  • consecutive hits by the same player not allowed (blocks are exempted)

Rally Ends

  • ball touches the ground
  • ball goes ‘out’
  • team fails to return ball
  • team commits a fault

Scoring

  • team winning a rally scores a point
  • if receiving team wins a rally:
    • they earn the right to serve | their players rotate one position clockwise
  • best of 3 or 5 games will win matches
  • non-deciding games:
    • 25 points to win | minimum 2-point advantage | no scoring cap
  • deciding games:
    • 15 points to win | minimum 2-point advantage | no scoring cap
